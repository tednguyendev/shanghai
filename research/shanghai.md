# Shanghai

## Spots

1. Xintiandi -- upscale dining, bars, and galleries in restored Shikumen buildings
2. Nanjing Road -- world's longest shopping street (5.5 km), great at night
3. Tianzifang (Taikang Road) -- artsy lanes with studios, cafes, and boutiques in old Shikumen houses
4. Shanghai Museum -- one of China's finest, 11 galleries (bronze, jade, ceramics, calligraphy)
5. Shanghai Disney Resort -- mainland China's first Disney park
   - Pirates of the Caribbean: Battle for the Sunken Treasure
   - TRON Lightcycle Power Run
   - Zootopia: Hot Pursuit
   - Soaring Over the Horizon
   - Budget: ~$155 (ticket $55 + food/souvenirs $100)
6. Zhujiajiao Ancient Water Town -- "Venice of Shanghai," 1,700+ years old
7. Jiashan Market -- old factory turned creative park, coffee shops, studios
8. Shanghai Circus World (ERA show) -- acrobat show, insane stunts
9. teamLab Borderless Shanghai -- 6,600 sqm digital art museum, 50+ interactive installations, boundary-less artworks
10. Bund Sightseeing Tunnel -- psychedelic 5-min light show tunnel under Huang Pu River, connects Bund to Pudong
11. Shanghai Natural History Museum -- dinosaur skeletons, mummies, nautilus-shaped building
12. Pudong Walk & Shanghai Tower -- tallest building in China, observation deck at 546m
13. The Bund -- iconic waterfront, colonial architecture, Huangpu River views
14. Shanghai Ocean Aquarium -- underwater tunnel, Yangtze River wildlife
15. Starbucks Reserve Roastery -- world's largest Starbucks, coffee theater experience

## Immersive Dining Experiences

### Palace Banquet Options
Both offer 80-90 min immersive shows with multi-course meals in ancient Chinese imperial court settings.

**Xuyan (叙宴)** - Tang Dynasty themed
- Location: Near Wenshui Rd (Metro Line 1)
- Theme: Chang'an City palace feast with modern light/tech
- Focus: Storytelling + immersive fantasy
- Lunch: ¥398-498 (~$57-71) | Dinner: ¥498-698 (~$71-99)
- Package types: Standard Seat / VIP / SVIP
- Add-ons: Hanfu costume, makeup, photography
- Best for: Tang Dynasty fans, tech/light show lovers
- Klook: https://www.klook.com/activity/141410-the-feast-ancient-chinese-immersive-palace-feast-shanghai-flagsh/

**Gong Yan (宫宴 / Sheng Gong Yan)** - Multi-dynasty
- Location: Oriental Art Palace
- Theme: Qin → Tang → Song dynasties
- Focus: Photo paradise with theatrical performances
- Lunch: ¥398-598 (~$57-85) | Dinner: ¥498-698 (~$71-99)
- More upscale/premium reputation
- Best for: Photography enthusiasts, multi-era variety

**Activity options:**
- Chang'an Fu vs All Nations Paying Tribute (different themed shows)
- Lunch Banquet vs Dinner Banquet (dinner more expensive, better ambiance)
- Standard/VIP/SVIP seating (affects view quality)
- Add-ons: Clothing (Hanfu), Makeup, Photography, Birthday Package

**Taste of China** - Tech-forward immersive dining
- Location: The Bund (Old City Hall, B1)
- 7-course meal with 270° panoramic screens
- Mountains and Yellow River projections around tables
- Sessions: 12:00-14:00 (lunch) / 19:00-21:00 (dinner)

**Dining Opera**
- Immersive dining with live opera performances

**Recommendation:** Add "Clothing + Makeup" package for full experience. Block 2-3 hours for the meal.

## Must-Try Dishes

1. Xiaolongbao -- soup dumplings
2. Sheng jian bao -- pan-fried buns, crispy bottom, soupy inside
3. Scallion oil noodles (葱油拌面) -- simple, incredible
4. Red-braised pork belly (红烧肉) -- sweet soy-braised, melt-in-mouth
5. Cong you bing -- scallion pancakes, flaky and crispy
6. Ci fan (糍饭) -- sticky rice rolls with fried dough, breakfast staple
7. Stinky tofu -- fermented, deep-fried
8. Shengjian mantou with crab roe -- upgraded sheng jian bao
9. Jianbing (煎饼) -- savory breakfast crepe from a street cart
